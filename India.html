Welcome to India

Overview

India, officially the Republic of India, is a country in South Asia. It is the seventh-largest country by area and the second-most populous country in the world.

Key Facts

  • Capital: New Delhi
  • Population: Over 1.4 billion
  • Official Languages: Hindi, English, and numerous others
  • Famous for: Diverse culture, history, cuisine, and monuments like the Taj Mahal

Popular Destinations

  • Taj Mahal, Agra
  • Jaipur, Rajasthan
  • Kerala Backwaters
  • Goa Beaches
